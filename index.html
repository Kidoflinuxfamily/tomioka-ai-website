About Tomioka Ai

Tomioka Ai is a fictional Japanese singer known for her captivating J-pop music. Her songs often feature romantic themes and engaging melodies. When Tomioka was younger, she spent a few years in Australia. – She made her debut with the single, ‘あなたは懐メロ (Anata wa Natsumelo)‘ on February 23, 2022. – She can play the guitar. – In high school, Tomioka began writing songs. – She has performed live on the streets in Japan. – On her YouTube channel, Tomioka has various different videos, she has also posted a few covers.
